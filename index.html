Data Wrangling

Posted on 2025-09-30 Edited on 2025-10-13

 In the world of data science, data is called “new oil”, but raw data is often not as neat and usable as one might expect. In reality, we often encounter missing values, duplicate records, inconsistent formats, abnormal data and even error messages. If these “dirty data” are directly used for analysis or modeling without processing, it may lead to distorted results and even affect the accuracy of decision-making. This is where the value of Data Wrangling lies - through a systematic approach, it cleans, transforms and integrates disordered raw data, ultimately forming structured and analyzable data sets. Data organization is not only the first step in the data science process, but also the cornerstone of the success of the entire analysis and modeling.
